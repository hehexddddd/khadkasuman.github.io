I have for the first time found what I can truly love—I have found you.
You are my sympathy—my better self—my good angel; I am bound to you with a strong attachment.
I think you are good, gifted, lovely: a fervent, a solemn passion is conceived in my heart;
it leans to you, draws you to my center and spring of life, wraps my existence about you—and,
kindling in pure, powerful flame, fuses you and me in one. – Charlotte Brontë
